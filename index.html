About Us

Experience Paradise on Saint Martin's Island

Nestled on the pristine shores of the Bay of Bengal, Mermaid Resort offers an unparalleled escape to Bangladesh's only coral island. Our resort combines natural beauty with modern luxury.

Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, an adventure of scuba diving in crystal-clear waters, or simply relaxation on pristine beaches, Mermaid Resort is your gateway to paradise.
